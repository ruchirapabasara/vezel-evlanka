⚙️ The Intelligent e:HEV Powertrain

The Vezel's advanced hybrid system automatically selects the most efficient drive mode for any situation, providing a seamless blend of power and fuel economy.

⚡

EV Drive

For low-speed driving, the Vezel runs silently on 100% electric power from the battery.

⚙️

Hybrid Drive

For acceleration, the engine powers a generator, which delivers optimum power to the electric motor for instant torque.

⛽

Engine Drive

At high, constant speeds, the engine connects directly to the wheels for maximum efficiency.

🛡️ Honda Sensing®: Your Intelligent Co-Pilot

Standard on every Vezel, Honda Sensing® is a suite of advanced safety and driver-assistive technologies designed to make driving easier and safer.

💥 Collision Prevention

  • ●
    Collision Mitigation Braking System™ (CMBS™): Helps brake the vehicle when it detects an unavoidable frontal collision.
  • ●
    Sudden Acceleration Suppression: Helps suppress engine output if the accelerator is applied suddenly from a stop with an obstacle ahead.
  • ●
    Cross Traffic Monitor (CTM): Z & PLaY Warns of approaching vehicles when reversing.

🚗 Driving Support

  • ●
    Adaptive Cruise Control (ACC) with Low-Speed Follow: Maintains a set speed and following distance, even in stop-and-go traffic.
  • ●
    Lane Keeping Assist System (LKAS): Provides steering input to help keep the vehicle centered in a detected lane.
  • ●
    Road Departure Mitigation (RDM): Helps steer you back into your lane if you begin to drift over detected lane markings without signaling.
  • ●
    Traffic Jam Assist: Provides steering assistance to help keep the vehicle centered at low speeds in congested traffic.

💡 Awareness & Visibility

  • ●
    Auto High-Beam Headlights: Automatically switches between high and low beams depending on traffic conditions.
  • ●
    Blind Spot Information (BSI): Z & PLaY Alerts you to vehicles detected in your blind spot when changing lanes.
  • ●
    Adaptive Driving Beam (ADB): Z & PLaY Intelligently shapes the high-beam to illuminate the road without dazzling other drivers.
  • ●
    Traffic Sign Recognition: Reminds you of posted road sign information, such as the current speed limit.
